UV Coaters, UV Coating Equipment


Walco UV Coating Machines – Direct Action

WALCO’s Coaters are the result of over 50 years of engineering for simple and reliable operation. These coaters are specifically designed for applying UV cured coatings to the top and/or bottoms of clean, flat and dry sheet stock, flexible webs, banners, posters and the like.

Walco’s UV coating equipment comes in both medium and heavy duty versions. All are available in whatever width meets your requirements, plus multiple standard operational and safety features.

Over 300+ upgrade modules allow Walco to tailor each machine specifically to meet your operational and safety requirements. No two units are alike–our design and engineering teams work with your team from the beginning to delivery to ensure that the machine you require is the one you receive. NO Surprises!
