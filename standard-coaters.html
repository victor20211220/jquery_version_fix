Automated Coating Machines: Industrial Roll Coaters


These automated liquid coaters (also called "roll coaters" and "roller coaters") are specifically designed for directly applying virtually any liquid coating to the top and/or bottoms of clean, flat and dry sheet stock, wood, glass, acrylic, plastic, sheet metal, webs, wovens, non-wovens and the like. Over 300+ upgrade modules allow Walco to tailor each machine specifically to meet your operational and safety requirements. WALCO’s Automated coating equipment is the result of over 50 years of engineering for simple and reliable operation.

Walco’s direct action series of automated liquid coaters come in both medium and heavy duty versions. All are available in whatever width meets your requirements, plus multiple standard operational and safety features.


Automated roll coating applications can include:

  • Glue Spreading
  • Clear Top Coating
  • Color Coating
  • Painting
  • Oil Coating
  • Reverse Coating
  • and more...

Precision adjustments allow exact metering of the coating liquid for uniform coating, saving time and money. The roll opening is adjustable – any thickness of stock from .001" to 1.5" (Special wide opening coaters are available). The coating liquid is poured into the reservoir formed by the coating roll and the doctor roll. By adjusting the doctor roll away from the coating roll, the thickness of the coating liquid is regulated.

Walco builds to your requirements; each unit is custom tailored based on your team's input from the design and all the way through manufacturing. Our design and engineering teams work with you to deliver a system that meets your operational and safety specifications–no surprises!

There are two Automated Coating Equipment versions available:


WALCO’S 60A Series Medium Duty Automated Roll Coater is a basic floor model mounted on a steel base that can be equipped with casters. The versatility of the WALCO automated liquid coater is proven by the thousands of machines in use all over the world. Walco builds to your requirements.

Operational, safety and clean-up upgrades are simple with Walco – helping your team to consistently produce better and better product, safely!.

Over 300+ upgrade modules are available, with the most popular including:

  • Scraper Blade – keeping the bottom drive roll clean.
  • Variable Speed Control – allowing easy slow down / speed up operations.
  • Anti-wrap Air Knifes – used top keep thin substrates or webs moving downstream as intended.
  • Automatic Coating Fill – automatically keeps your coating liquid filled at the crotch. All you need to do is make certain your coating bucket is full.
  • Removable UHMW end dams – for very quick cleanup
  • PLC / HMI automation, including automated cleaning (Water based coating materials only)
  • External Safety walls and/or light curtain
