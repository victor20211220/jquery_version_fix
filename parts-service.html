WALCO, Inc. Replacement Parts


In the unlikely event that your Walco industrial laminator, coater, or applicator machine or system needs parts, you can count on Walco to get you get you operational quickly. Walco keeps a good stock of parts and accessories ready to service our customers’ replacement part requirements.

Even with over 10,400 Walco systems or machines shipped worldwide, Walco keeps extensive documentation of each machine. Although each machine’s record is detailed by a serial number, if that’s not available some simple photos can likely help our team determine what is needed. Walco technicians work to get you the part you need when you need it.

WALCO Service and Refurbishment

Walco’s Service Team will help keep your laminator, coater, or applicator machine or system running as long as you need it. Our location is near major air and rail facilities so getting your part (or us) to you can be done very expeditiously. Typically each service or refurbishment evaluation call is divided into two phases:

Phase 1 includes the initial site visit, operational evaluation and formal repair or refurbishment proposal. Standard Phase 1 costs may include*:

  • Walco on-site technician for service or refurbishment evaluation $1,360.00 per day M-F, 7:30 am to 4: 30 pm.
  • Travel time @ the same rate.
  • Alternate times, overtime and additional days @ Walco’s standard labor rate sheet (available on request).

*Please note - Because travel costs such as airfare, lodging, meals, and other travel related expenses fluctuate, they are billed additionally.

Phase 2 is to accomplish the proposed work. All Phase 1 costs are credited to the Phase 2 work.

All members of the Walco Parts and Service team have many years of experience building and servicing Walco, Inc.. Allow us to use this experience to assist in keeping your Walco Machinery functioning at its best

Contact Walco or Request a Quote today: Walco’s expert Parts and Service team is standing by to support your laminating, coating, or converting systems’ replacement parts or service and refurbishment requirements.
