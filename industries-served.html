Any of Walco's machines or systems are custom tailored to customer operational and safety requirements. Length, width, thickness, speed, temperature, pressure, materials, weight of coating, and/or finish are able to be incorporated into the specific design for any equipment order.

Through back and forth exchanges a full system requirement is generated, allowing customers to fully review prior to fabrication, ensuring no surprises on delivery.

Your new Walco machine or system is an investment in your business’s success; custom tailoring ensures your investment is maximized.
