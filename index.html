Industrial Laminating Machines, Industrial Coating Machines, Web Converting Equipment


Walco Inc. is now part of the Chargeurs Group, a global manufacturing and service group with leading positions in the protective film, technical substrates, interlining and combed wool industries. Walco’s three (3) manufacturing facilities join the 360 employees in over 60 countries on 5 continents, securing your needs.

Walco continues to provide our clients with the highest level of service and equipment it has since 1949. Walco's legendary equipment is:

  • Robust: Walco has been designing and building robust coating and industrial laminating systems, web-to-web converting, and application systems since 1949. Over 10,300 machines have been shipped to 60+ countries.
  • World-Class: Walco is proud to offer the best equipment in the industry, worldwide.
  • Safe: Walco’s reputation for building systems with safety and quality has remained unparalleled for many decades.
  • Simple-to-use: Walco is proud to offer a line of equipment that will get the job done with simplicity, allowing your team to easily train, operate and maintain your equipment for many years.

Walco, Inc. is big enough to have shipped the world's largest heavy duty laminating machine, but small and nimble enough to pay special attention to your requirements. You will receive a laminating, coating, applicator or web converting machine to fit your specific needs and environment. Approximately 80% of all Walco industrial lamination systems are variations on Walco’s proven designs. Walco can design a complete, fully automated, customized production line, or easily incorporate proven Walco legacy systems to work together seamlessly.


Industrial Coating and Laminating Machines Designed to Last


For decades, Walco has consistently emphasized simplicity of design, quality, and ease of operation. With all Walco, Inc. machines- Safety is first. Many standard safety features are included on every Walco machine. The durability and quality of Walco coating and laminating machinery guarantees customers many years of trouble-free operation. As a result, thousands of Walco machines are still in service after decades of use.

No unit or system is simply "out of the box." Walco tailors each system to our client's actual operational and safety requirements.

This flexibility has earned us reputation as a lamination machine manufacturer with functional and highly reliable heavy duty laminators, wide format laminators, coaters, applicators and web converters tailored to meet our customer's specific requirements.

  • Capability to work with many surfaces and materials
  • Capacity to build special purpose system machines
  • Ability to provide complete turnkey, stand-alone or inline systems
  • Capability for fully automated, HMI integrated or Operator controlled systems
  • Flexibility in design to allow quick retrofits
  • Ability to provide all needed in and outfeed conveyor requirement
