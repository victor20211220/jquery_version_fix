Walco Model 82-C-M1 Industrial Overlay Laminator


The WALCO Model 82-C-1M Sheet-to-sheet Laminator is the most popular model we have ever built. Designed for sheet fed operation, this series is available to 62" (152 cm) width. A full range of accessories, from silicone covered rolls to variable speed drives, make it possible to laminate virtually any overlay material to sheet substrates with minimum set-up time.

How this Sheet-to-sheet Laminator Works


Sheet-to-sheet lamination is easy. Precision roll opening adjustment allows laminating to soft substrates as well as rigid. Foot pedal operation is standard. If an interliner must be removed, an automatic rewind should be ordered with the machine. Shown here is our standard model.

As with all Walco Machines laminating machines we'll tailor your overlay laminator system to meet your exact needs and requirements. From the beginning to the end, Walco design and engineering teams are in contact to make sure you receive a unit manufactured to your precise specifications–No surprises at delivery!
