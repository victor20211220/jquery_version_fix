Glass Laminating Machines, Glass Coating Equipment



    Walco's line of Glass Laminating Systems apply any PSA protective or permanent films to the top and / or bottom of Glass, Acrylic, Solar and Plastic products. IGU (Insulated Glass Unit) and Glass lites are Walco's specialty.


    No two units are the same; each glass laminator or coater is custom tailored to our customers' specific lamination needs. From the beginning of the quoting and design process to manufacturing, our design and engineering teams work hand in hand with your teams to ensure we meet your actual operational and safety requirements.

    With a base of 21 standard systems and over 300+ operational modules, Walco will manufacture the glass, acrylic or solar lamination system to meet your operational requirements.

     

    Walco's line of glass lamination equipment is:

  • Available in widths from 6.0" to 156.0 inches
  • Inline or Offline
  • Full automatic, manual, or anything in between
  • Fully integrated into your existing glass line.
  • Walco Laminators

    •   Inline Film to Glass – Start/Stop Operation Video

Walco's Film to Glass laminating systems are specifically designed to apply a wide range of PSA films to glass lites, IGU's, acrylic and solar panels.

As a world wide top lamination machine manufacturer, Walco understand the critical production requirements of the glass and acrylic industry. Our machines have long been the industry standard for glass laminating equipment.

In addition, Walco offers a protective film laminator designed specifically for Insulated Glass Units. A fully automated vertical laminator, the VX2 has patented technologies allowing it to apply films to IGU’s for protection during construction installation.
